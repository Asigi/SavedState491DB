Explanation

The balls will move around and try to form cliques.
  • When a clique is formed, the balls will move around together.
  • Note that the balls are not racist. They don't let color get in the way of friendship.
  • Also, larger balls dominate and sometimes move on top of the smaller ones.
Sometimes cliques fight.
  • This might result in loyalty changes (a ball joins the other clique).
Note that a lone ball is capable of stealing members from a clique.
